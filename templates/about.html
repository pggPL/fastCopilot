About

The copilot is based on LSTM neural network, which predicts the completion of the sentence based on the previous words. Different models were trained on the different datasets.

Model name Description Dataset
Linux Source Small 4 layers, 128 hidden neurons files from the Linux source
Polish Small 4 layers, 128 hidden neurons concatenated polish sentences.
Poems Small 4 layers, 128 hidden neurons concatenated english poems.
